    Team Booking Category Configuration

    Your Team:

    From this page, you as a teamleader can manage which booking categories your employees can assign to their dice. Check any categories that are relevant for your team.

    Booking Categories

    The global pool of booking categories is managed by your administrators. Selecting those relevant for your team ensures employees can only use these categories when assigning the sides of their dice.
